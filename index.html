Frequently Asked Questions

What is n8n and why should I use it?
n8n is a powerful, open-source workflow automation tool that allows businesses to connect apps, automate processes, and integrate AI without writing complex code. It's more flexible and cost-effective than alternatives like Zapier or Make.
How long does it take to implement automation?
Simple workflows can be deployed in 1-2 weeks, while complex enterprise solutions may take 4-8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase based on your specific requirements.
Do you provide training for our team?
Yes! All our packages include comprehensive documentation, and Professional and Enterprise plans include hands-on training sessions to ensure your team can manage and optimize the workflows.
Can you integrate with our existing tools?
Absolutely! n8n supports hundreds of integrations out of the box, and we can create custom integrations for any tool with an API. We work with CRMs, databases, marketing tools, and more.
What kind of ROI can I expect?
Most clients see ROI within 3-6 months through time savings, reduced errors, and increased productivity. On average, businesses save 20-40 hours per week and reduce operational costs by 30-50%.
Do you offer ongoing support after implementation?
Yes, all packages include support ranging from 30 to 90 days. We also offer extended support contracts and ongoing optimization services for Enterprise clients.
