Frequently Asked Questions

What is n8n and why is it better than other automation tools? ▼

n8n is a fair-code licensed workflow automation tool that stands out for its flexibility, self-hosting capabilities, and extensive integration options. Unlike many SaaS automation platforms, n8n gives you full control over your data and workflows. It's particularly powerful for complex business processes that require custom logic and multiple system integrations.

How long does it typically take to implement an automation solution? ▼

Implementation timelines vary based on complexity. Simple workflows can be delivered in 2-3 weeks, while comprehensive automation systems typically take 4-8 weeks. We follow an agile approach, delivering working components incrementally so you start seeing value quickly.

Do you provide training and ongoing support? ▼

Yes, comprehensive training and documentation are included with all our packages. We also offer ongoing support plans to help you maintain and optimize your automation systems. Our goal is to make you self-sufficient while providing expert assistance when needed.

Can you integrate with our existing systems and software? ▼

Absolutely! n8n has built-in integrations with hundreds of popular services, and we can create custom connections for any system with an API. We've integrated with everything from legacy on-premise systems to modern cloud platforms.

What kind of ROI can we expect from automation? ▼

Most clients achieve ROI within 3-6 months. Typical results include 60-80% reduction in manual processing time, elimination of human error, and significant cost savings. Use our ROI calculator above to estimate potential savings for your specific situation.
